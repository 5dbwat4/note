## Chapter 10.7 Planar Graphs

- **Kuratowski’s theorem**: A graph is nonplanar if and only if it contains a subgraph homeomorphic to $K_{3,3}$ or $K_5$.
- 库拉托夫斯基定理：一个图是非平面的当且仅当它包含一个与$K_{3,3}$或$K_5$同胚的子图。
